About me

I make stuff. Since around 2003 I've mostly been making intangible things such as websites, software, and music, but now I'm getting into making physical things like speakers, essential oil displays, and other simple products. I also make art, but haven't had the courage to share much of it.

I'm known for being able to take an idea for a product or service and turn it into a business. My friends, colleagues, and partners share their ideas with me and I help them figure out how to make it a reality. I'm currently working on turning this type of input/output into a company, HatchMatter, where everyone involved has a unique skillset that can bring more ideas to life.
